Architectural Application

CONDITIONS OF APPROVAL:

Applicant understands and agrees that this Application does not fulfill all of the conditions and requirements for  an approval. In addition to this completed “Architectural Application” form, the applicant must submit to the  Architectural Committee drawings and specifications showing the nature, kind, shape, height, and materials and  locations of the proposed architectural alteration. The submission must be in writing. The Architectural  Committee may require additional information, as it deems necessary to make a decision. Until all information is  submitted to the committee, the application will be deemed to be incomplete and the application will stand  disapproved.

Applicant further understands and agrees that in the event that the modification is approved by the Committee that they may impose “Special Conditions” of construction and maintenance on the approval of the Application. Any special conditions shall be attached and be a part of the approval. Any deviation from the approved plans specifications or special conditions will cause the permit to automatically terminate and become null and void. Applicant understands and agrees that if applicant fails to conform to each requirement then applicant authorizes De Anza Oaks Homeowners Association to have the work brought into conformance with approved plans, specifications and special conditions. Applicant further understands and agrees that all costs incurred by the Association as a result of bringing said work into compliance will be a personal obligation of the owner. Such costs shall include the costs of construction, reconstruction, administration, fees, attorney fees, experts and consultants charges, and court costs, if incurred. It is also agreed that no work will (1) be initiated which will be a violation of any of the provisions of the Association’s Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions or any applicable building code, (2) be an annoyance to any resident; or (3) increase the cost of insurance maintained by the Association.

It is further agreed that, in the event the application is approved, all maintenance, repair or replacement of the  approved item will be the sole responsibility of the lot owner, and further that any expense incurred by the  Association that is the direct or indirect result of the approved change shall also be the sole responsibility of the  lot owner. All work shall be initiated with consideration of the terrain, slope, esthetics, time and noise  considerations.

Applicant understands that all necessary permits and approvals from applicable governmental jurisdictions are the sole responsibility of the applicant and that Committee approval of this application is subject to the applicant  receiving all such necessary permits and approvals.

Applicant agrees to use licensed and insured contractors for any and all installations or to make the Architectural Committee aware when this is the exception. Applicant agrees to notify the Architectural Committee upon  completion of the project so the Committee may inspect and determine compliance with each and all of the  conditions of approval of the application.
