Facilities

De Anza Oaks offers a playground and plenty of green space for families as well as a 25 yard heated swimming pool and children's pool. Bathrooms equipped with hot showers are located near the pool. Our clubhouse is perfect for entertaining with it's newly remodeled interior. For further information, please go to the Clubhouse description in Facilities.
Image
Clubhouse
The clubhouse has a kitchen, formal area, electric fireplace and two bathrooms and deck. It can seat a maximum of 72 people as established by the fire department. There is a $100 deposit which will be returned to the renter if no damage has occurred and if the clubhouse has been cleaned in a satisfactory manner, subject to the criteria of the clubhouse chairperson.

De Anza Oaks Pools
De Anza Oaks has a heated 25 yard pool and a heated children's pool available for the enjoyment of the homeowners and their tenants. Admittance to the pool is with a key card and lot identification tag only.
